About E-Conclave

E-Conclave, the entrepreneurship conference of IIT Bombay, is organized by E-Club, IIT Bombay, which is the premier entrepreneurship student body for the postgraduate and doctoral students of IIT Bombay. We have seen the attendance of veteran speakers like Virendra Gupta (DailyHunt), Vasudevan Chinnathambi (Ninjakart) in our past events. The grand E-Conclave 2021 is scheduled to take place in November 2021. Top entrepreneurs of the country are set to discuss their tryst with entrepreneurship and achievements in the conference. IIT Bombay is all set to witness multiple fireside chats and keynotes on 20th and 21st November during E-Conclave.
Benefits of attending this:

  • Networking Sessions
  • Meet New Professional Faces
  • Get Inspired by Amazing Speakers
  • Receive tips on how to begin your entrepreneurial journey
